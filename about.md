# About

Tech News Digest is a personal news roundup. Every couple of days, the latest stories from a curated list of credible tech publications get pulled together into a single short read.

## How it's made

The digests are assembled with AI assistance. Articles are pulled from the RSS feeds of established tech publishers, summarized down to a few sentences each, and grouped by topic. Every story links back to the original article so you can read the full piece from the publisher who reported it.

AI summaries can occasionally miss nuance or get details wrong — if something looks off, the original article is always one click away.

## Sources & rights

Each item is a short excerpt with a link to the original. **All rights remain with the original publishers.** Nothing here is reproduced in full, and the goal is to send readers *toward* the publications doing the reporting, not to replace them.

If you're a publisher and would like an item removed or attributed differently, get in touch via the Instagram below.
